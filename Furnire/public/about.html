Our Story

Founded in 2010, Furniro began as a small workshop with a big dream - to create beautiful, high-quality furniture that transforms houses into homes. What started as a passion project between two friends has grown into a trusted brand known for craftsmanship and design excellence.

Today, we're proud to serve customers across the country with our curated collection of furniture and home accessories. Each piece in our collection is thoughtfully designed to combine functionality with timeless style.

We believe that great furniture should be accessible to everyone, which is why we work directly with artisans and manufacturers to bring you exceptional quality at fair prices.
